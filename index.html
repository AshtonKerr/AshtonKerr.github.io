Packing Information

Before Packing

Liquids, gels and aerosols packed in carry-on must follow the 3-1-1 liquids rule: 3.4 ounces or less per container 1 quart size, clear, plastic, zip top bag (all liquids must fit in bag) 1 bag per passenger Review the prohibited items list for both carry-on and checked baggage. If purchasing a baggage lock, be sure to look for those that are TSA recognized. Tape a card with your name and contact information on your electronics.

When Packing

Pack items in layers (shoes one layer, clothes one layer, electronics one layer, etc.) Pack large electronics on top layer of carry-on for screening accessibility. Place your 3-1-1 bag with liquids, gels and aerosols in the front pocket of your carry-on for accessibility. If traveling with a pet, be sure to bring a leash so carriers can be properly screened.

Before Leaving for the Airport

Give yourself enough time to arrive at the airport early. Wear easily removable shoes. Passengers with a disability or medical condition may call ahead.

In Standard Screening Lane

Remove the 3-1-1 liquids bag and place it in the bin. Ensure pockets are empty (keys, tissues, currency, wallets, cell phones, etc.) and remove bulky jewelry (valuable items can be placed in carry-on). Remove your shoes and place them directly on the X-ray belt. Remove personal electronic devices larger than a cell phone from your carry-on bag and place them into a bin with nothing placed on or under them for X-ray screening. (E.g. laptops, tablets, e-readers and handheld game consoles.) Remember to check the bins and collect all belongings after going through screening.

Top Tips for staying at Riu Republica

Tip #1

The resort is about 30% full due to less travelers, go use the back half of the resort, we had Two Large Pool all to our selfs, there is no bar so bring cups and fill your drinks for the bar near the lobby

Tip #2

Since only some restaurants are open per day, pick which ones your really want to go to an day One, else you might miss them. The Mexican and Indian restaurants in the back half were always closed but the most popular ones are open for sure, just not every day

Tip #3

Do NOT pick restaurants based on the schedule in the app, the app is not updated, take a photo of each schedule as you walk by them

Tip #4

If a Rain Storm comes by and just happens to be blowing the right way, YES you will get a beach full of sea weed, it will take them 1-2 days to clear it. For us Days 1-3 had a perfect white sand beach and water, Days 4 and 5 was full of sea weed due to the very late night storm. The resort is on the ocean, sea weed can happen to all resorts

Tip #5

Late night food and bar. This is an adult only resort, most go to bed by midnight so only the 24 hr bar is open with just Hamburgers and Hot Dogs, Chips and fruit, nothing else other than tons of alcohol

Tip #6

If your room is hot shut the blinds, acts as insulation. make sure patio door is closed else air shuts off. When you first arrive turn on air and set to lowest , it was off for us

Tip #7

If bath room smells run water down sink and shower when your first get there, Depends on seward lines

Tip #8

If you see a Cat, it speaks Spanish so I think it is not Here Kitty Kitty Kitty, but Here Gato Gato Gato and he comes a running, wants food. dont feed him, he is very loud

Tip #9

Window in bathroom is frosted glass can see through it somewhat, if you dont like this bring roll of Tape and cover shower window with a sheet

Tip #10

If want anything like more beer, sheets, pillows, blankets fix toilet, just push a button the RUI app and they come a running

Tip #11

All in all great trip, best food is in the speciality restaurants not the buffet. For best Drinks order a Drink AND a shot on the side add it to your drink to get a USA level of drink else they are watered down. Bring lots of TIP money as most were out of work for a year there plus you get better drink if you tip every time

Tip #12

Photo Copy your Passport and avoid the line for photo copying for covid testing skip the check in line and go to Room 318 20 mins before the scheduled time. It will be very hot there with no AC but it only took 20 mins For those from the USA, your COVID Test takes 30 Hours to get the email back, check your spam box, confirmed them with others you also took 30 hours to get their email the day before

Tip #13

Remind Ashton if he forgets but get him to sign into his VPN on everyones devices. It's really easy to steal information, including financials when using unsecured WIFI's at resorts. If you're using a VPN it's untraceable!
